## Overview  

**Mac Blu-ray Player** is a versatile and reliable media playback software designed to provide seamless Blu-ray and DVD experiences on macOS. It supports playback of Blu-ray discs, folders, and ISO image files with full HD and UHD video quality, ensuring an exceptional viewing experience on any Mac device.  

The software features **full disc navigation**, allowing users to access menus, chapters, and special features just like on physical discs. It is compatible with a wide range of audio and video formats, including MKV, MP4, AVI, FLAC, AAC, and more, offering a complete multimedia solution.  

With **Mac Blu-ray Player**, you can enjoy 3D Blu-ray movies, lossless audio tracks, and customizable subtitle options. The application supports external Blu-ray drives and is optimized for Intel and Apple Silicon Macs, delivering smooth performance without high CPU usage.  

Whether you are watching movies, streaming content from discs, or enjoying high-fidelity audio, Mac Blu-ray Player ensures a professional and enjoyable media playback experience on MacBook, MacBook Pro, and iMac devices.
